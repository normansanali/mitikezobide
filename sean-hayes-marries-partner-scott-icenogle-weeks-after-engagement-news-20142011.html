Sean Hayes Marries Partner Scott Icenogle Weeks After Engagement News

August 17, 2024
blog

Hope Jack had a dance party! Sean Hayes secretly wed his longtime partner Scott Icenogle last week. The Will & Grace actor shared the news on his official Facebook page on Thursday, Nov. 20.

"Here's a #TBT photo of Scotty and me getting married last week. Took us 8 years but we did it!" the 44-year-old captioned a pic of the newlyweds.

Us confirmed late last month that Hayes was set to wed the music composer after eight years of dating. He first sparked buzz of an engagement when he appeared on The Ellen DeGeneres show wearing a silver band on Oct. 24.

The Sean Saves the World alum came out as gay in 2010 in The Advocate, where he briefly opened up about Icenogle, although he didn't identify him by name. "I spend time with a special someone in my life," he told the mag at the time. "That's it. That's all I need. I don't need events. I don't do a lot. I live my life like an 85-year-old man. I'm just quiet. It's fantastic."
