Monopoly Go Jungle Jam Event: Starting Date, Rewards &amp; Task List

July 26, 2024
blog

Monopoly Go! Jungle Jam, the exciting mobile game that combines Monopoly and Coin Master elements, is creating a buzz on Android and iOS devices. With thousands of downloads and a growing fan base, players are diving into this unique gaming experience.

When is the Jungle Jam Event Happening?

The Monopoly Go Jungle Jam event is currently underway, and there’s a lot to discover about it. This event started on August 26, 2023, and will continue until August 28, 202It’s a two-day extravaganza featuring a whopping 42 milestones.

Exploring the Jungle Jam Rewards

In the Jungle Jam event, your goal is to roll the dice and collect Hats scattered across the game board. Each time you land on a tile with Hats, you’ll earn two points. To progress through the 42 milestones, you’ll need to collect a specific number of Hats. The rewards await you as you complete these milestones.

Here’s a glimpse of the rewards you can earn up to the 20th milestone

Milestone 1: Collect 5 points and receive Cash.

Milestone 2: Gather 10 points for 10 free dice rolls.

Milestone 3: Reach 10 points for a Green Sticker Pack.

Milestone 4: Accumulate 80 points for a generous 125 free dice rolls.

Milestone 5: Get 15 points for more Cash.

Milestone 6: Achieve 20 points and earn a Green Sticker Pack.

Milestone 7: Secure 25 points for additional Cash.

Milestone 8: Amass 150 points for a remarkable 220 free dice rolls.

Milestone 9: Reach 25 points and be rewarded with Cash.

Milestone 10: Hit 30 points for a 15-minute Rent Frenzy.

Milestone 11: Collect 35 points for a pack of Green Stickers.

Milestone 12: Gain 40 points and receive Cash.

Milestone 13: Gather a substantial 425 points for a whopping 550 free dice rolls.

Milestone 14: Secure 45 points for another pack of Green Stickers.

Milestone 15: Achieve 50 points for Cash.

Milestone 16: Reach 55 points and enjoy a 15-minute Rent Frenzy.

Milestone 17: Amass 800 points for a generous 900 free dice rolls.

Milestone 18: Collect 60 points for Cash.

Milestone 19: Hit 70 points and earn another pack of Green Stickers.

Milestone 20: Secure 80 points for more Cash.

As you progress beyond the 20th milestone, the requirements become even more challenging.

Unveiling the Jungle Jam Task List

The Jungle Jam event also features a task list that keeps evolving. Some tasks, such as Free Parking, Peg E, Cash Boost, and others, make regular appearances. However, some events are rare and may only happen once in a while. These events offer great opportunities to stock up on dice, so make sure to seize them when they come your way.

Remember, you might have to wait for your dice to refresh or consider purchasing some if you run out during these events. It’s essential to conserve your dice because using a 5x or 10x multiplier will consume a corresponding number of dice. However, these multipliers also grant you 5x or 10x Hats, which can significantly boost your progress in the game.
