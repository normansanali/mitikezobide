Recap: What Happened in The Tale Of The Outcasts Episode 8?

Wisteria and Marbas have finally reached London in their search for Wisteria’s missing brother, Snow. However, Luther, the villainous mastermind behind Snow’s kidnapping, has unleashed a horde of zombies on the city.
