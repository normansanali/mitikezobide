Chloe Bennet Claims She's Not Dating Austin Nichols

August 02, 2024
blog

Just friends? Chloe Bennet is keeping mum about her relationship with former One Tree Hill star Austin Nichols! The 21-year-old actress is denying about a possible romance, despite rumors that the two are an item.

"Ah no, we're good friends," the Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. star told Us Weekly at the Teen Vogue Young Hollywood party in Los Angeles, Calif., on Friday, Sept. 27. "We met in L.A. just hanging out," she said.

The former Nashville guest star was reportedly said to be dating Nichols, 33, earlier this month. "He's a very nice guy," she added. "He's a cutie."
