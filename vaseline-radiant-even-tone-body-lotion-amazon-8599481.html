The Vaseline Radiant X Even Tone Nourishing Body Lotion is a smoothing, tone-improving body lotion. The lightweight formula improves rough skin instantly; over time, ingredients — including dermatologist-loved niacinamide — improve tone by fading discoloration, in as little as two weeks, per the brand. Ultimately, expect a silky feel, a luminous sheen, and a buttery, even toned appearance from décolletage to feet with continued use. 

The formula’s key hyperpigmentation-thwarting, tone-improving power comes from niacinamide — a B vitamin that’s garnered ample acclaim, including from dermatologists like Dr. Hadley King, M.D. Niacinamide can “reduce some signs of skin aging,” Dr. King previously shared, especially as it relates to tone. Specifically, the ingredient fades skin discoloration, including sunspots and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, to deliver a more uniformed appearance.

Glycerin, a hydrating humectant, plumps skin on contact, and retains moisture for hours on end. As dermatologist Dr. Melanie Palm, M.D., previously told InStyle, "Glycerin is one of a precious few moisturizing ingredients that has a lasting effect, well beyond its time of application and absorption.”

Ceramides strengthen the skin barrier — an essential component to retaining moisture and preventing its escape — a phenomenon known as transepidermal water loss.  

Finally, coconut oil contains fatty acids that smooth the skin and maintain a supple look and feel. The ingredient boasts “anti-inflammatory and hydrating properties,” too, dermatologist Dr. Mona Gohara, M.D., previously shared with InStyle, and may calm inflammatory skin conditions such as eczema. 

Shoppers are shocked by how rapidly the body lotion delivers on its tone-improving claims. For instance, one reviewer, who purchased the cream prior to a sunny vacation hoping to “even out a few dark spots on [their] legs,” says they’ve noticed results “after only a week.” According to another shopper, who had struggled with post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation on their legs, the formula has notably improved unevenness. ”I’ve been looking for [a] lotion like this for years,” they conclude. Finally, yet another fan comments on the “nice sheen” it imparts in seconds.
