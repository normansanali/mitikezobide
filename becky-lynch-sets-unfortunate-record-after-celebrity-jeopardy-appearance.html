Becky Lynch Sets Unfortunate Record After Celebrity Jeopardy Appearance

August 24, 2024
blog

Becky Lynch’s hard work in WWE has paid off in many ways, as she is a multi-time women’s champion. Lynch has proved just how good she really is, but that didn’t translate to a good performance during her recent ‘Celebrity Jeopardy’ appearance. In fact, she ended up making history at the show for all the wrong reasons.

Big Time Becks competed against Hollywood heavyweights Macaulay Culkin and Rachel Dratch on the November 15th edition of ‘Celebrity Jeopardy.’ Fans were excited to see what she could do during the episode. As it turns out, she didn’t really do much of anything.

As pointed out by the Twitter account that keeps track of everything that goes on in the show, Becky Lynch made history as she is now the first contestant to never give a single correct answer through 60 clues. On the other hand, Macaulay Culkin got 25 correct answers and Rachel Dratch 20.

”Yes, this is believed to the first instance of a player giving 0 correct responses through 60 clues. #Jeopardy #CelebrityJeopardy”

Becky Lynch was also replaced on prominent WWE graphic recently. She is also likely to be the final member of Bianca Belair’s team for the Women’s WarGames match. We’ll have to see whether WWE will end up making the announcement soon.
