General Hospital Spoilers: Week of January 29 Willows Stunning News Sonnys Torn Heart

August 29, 2024
blog

General Hospital (GH) spoilers for the week of January 29-February 2 reveal Hamilton Finn’s (Michael Easton) medical malpractice trial will wrap up after Martin Grey (Michael E. Knight) presents major evidence. Will it be enough to convince the jury that Finn should be cleared of any wrongdoing?

While Finn faces the verdict, Nina Corinthos (Cynthia Watros) will mull over an offer from Valentin Cassadine (James Patrick Stuart) and ultimately strike a deal with him.

With Valentin in her corner, Nina should be able to take the first steps toward getting revenge on Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) and Drew Cain (Cameron Mathison).

Of course, Willow Corinthos (Katelyn MacMullen) will need to get updates on Drew firing Nina and replacing her with Carly at Crimson.

Will Willow think it’s what Nina deserves or object to yet another round of this feud?

Regardless, Willow may warn Nina not to retaliate – that is if she can stand to be in the same room long enough to deliver that warning!

As for Michael Corinthos (Chad Duell), he’ll continue to hold out hope that he can repair his damaged marriage, but Willow won’t make that easy.

In Paris, Spencer Cassadine (Nicholas Alexander Chavez) will romance Trina Robinson (Tabyana Ali) as they kick off their new adventure together, but they’d better brace for some danger.

GH spoilers say risky faceoff with Esme Prince (Avery Pohl) is coming up at some point – and it looks like it could leave Spencer presumed deceased sooner or later.

Meanwhile, Jordan Ashford (Tanisha Harper) will continue her investigation into the rifle used to fire at Curtis Ashford (Donnell Turner) despite the FBI’s threats.

Anna Devane (Finola Hughes) and Brick (Stephen A. Smith) will help Jordan, but they’ll all face some shockers that they didn’t see coming eventually.

As for Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard), it appears Diane Miller (Carolyn Hennesy) will finish cleaning up his latest legal mess, but she can’t fix his broken heart.

Sonny will remain hurt and angry, but he could also have a little compassion once he learns Nina lost Crimson. That’s especially true since Ava Jerome (Maura West) is living with Sonny and can help him see things Nina’s way.

Finally, Cody Bell (Josh Kelly) and Sasha Gilmore (Sofia Mattsson) will join forces to play Cupid for Maxie Jones (Kirsten Storms) and Damian Spinelli (Bradford Anderson), so that’ll bring some cute matchmaker moments and a chance to pull Sasha closer to Cody, too.

General Hospital spoilers say the week of January 29-February 2 will bring some excitement to Port Charles, so stay tuned for all the chaos and surprising news ahead.
