Kim Kardashian Posts Photo of Triplets Chicago, True, Stormi

July 25, 2024
blog

The next generation! Kim Kardashian posted the first photo of 8-month-old old daughter Chicago West posing next to cousins True Thompson, 5 months, and Stormi Webster, 7 months, to Instagram on Friday, September 14, and the shot couldn’t be more adorable.

“Triplets,” the KKW Beauty founder, 37, captioned the super cute pic, which showed the three babies gazing up in wonder at the camera.

While the snap marked the first time the reality star’s daughter has been pictured with her cousins, it was not her first playdate: On Tuesday, September 11, Kylie Jenner posted a super sweet picture of her daughter, Stormi (whom she shares with Travis Scott) and Chicago, who were born just 17 days apart, cuddled up in matching pink pajamas.

The Selfish author also shared a pic of Chicago reaching back toward True, who is amassing a rather darling online baby album of her own, just one week prior.

As Khloé Kardashian revealed in a sneak peek of Keeping Up With the Kardashians, she is leaning toward choosing Kim as her daughter True’s legal guardian.

“I am going to probably have Kim as the legal guardian if, God forbid, something happens,” she said in the clip of her only child with boyfriend Tristan Thompson. “Watching Kimberly be a mom, I really respect her parenting skills.”

“It is my honor, and I would take such good care of your kids,” Kim said in response to the news.
