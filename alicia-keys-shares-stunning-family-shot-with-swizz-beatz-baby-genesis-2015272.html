Alicia Keys Shares Stunning Family Shot With Swizz Beatz, Baby Genesis

August 03, 2024
blog

Gorgeous! Alicia Keys revealed the first photo of her new baby Genesis in a stunning family portrait posted to Instagram on Friday, Feb. 27.

The "Girl on Fire" singer, 34, shared a beautiful black-and-white portrait of herself with her husband Swizz Beatz and their children, including the latest addition. 

 

"Blessings and love!! #GenesisAliDean," Keys captioned the shot. The Grammy winner holds her newborn son, 2 months, in the photo, posing in an ornate embellished gown and a light-colored headwrap. 

Keys' husband of nearly five years sits by her side, smiling as he gazes at his son. Born Kasseem Dean, Swizz Beatz, 36, cozies up to his older son Kasseem Dean Jr., who is almost 7 years old. The little boy is dressed in a tuxedo, leaning over his father as he checks out his baby brother.
