Frankie Delgado Married: Former Hills Star Marries Jennifer Acosta

September 02, 2024
blog

Another Hills star is hitched! A week after his former Hills co-star Kristin Cavallari tied the knot to Jay Cutler, Frankie Delgado has made his love official. 

Delgado, married his girlfriend Jennifer Acosta on Saturday, June 15, Us Weekly can confirm.

The nuptials, which were attended by guests Lauren Conrad, Brody Jenner and Reggie Bush, took place at Rancho Las Lomas near Dana Point, Calif.

During the reception, Conrad, 27, posted a picture of the newlyweds dancing, writing, “Congratulations! @kingfrankiedelgado and @shopatrend (Jen) are officially husband and wife! So happy for you both xo.” In the picture, Delgado, clad in a Tom Ford suit, is joined by his gorgeous bride, who wore a wedding gown by designer Ines DiSanto.
