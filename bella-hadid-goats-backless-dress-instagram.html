Bella Hadid Poses with Goats in Photo Taken by Sister Gigi Hadid

September 14, 2024
blog
  • Bella Hadid shared two photos of herself cuddling up to Dua Lipa and Anwar Hadid's goats, named Funky and Bam-Bam.
  • Bella's sister Gigi shot the incredible photos, which are outtakes from a feature for i-D.

While lockdown life has made work more difficult for people all over the globe, Bella Hadid has been finding unique ways to continue her modeling career remotely.

Having already posed naked at home for Jacquemus, and displayed plenty of underboob in a plethora of bikinis, Bella decided to up the ante for her next shoot. So it should come as absolutely no surprise that the model decided to frolic around with two goats called Funky and Bam-Bam.

For the shoot, Bella wore a cozy $2,890 Bottega Veneta silk knit dress, which beautifully coordinated with her farmyard friends. In her Instagram caption, Bella also revealed that the super-cute goats belong to her brother, Anwar Hadid, and his girlfriend, Dua Lipa.

"thank you to their lovely parents ... for allowing them to take place in such a huge production. they were absolutely charming / great work ethic," she revealed in the caption. And, yes, Bella referred to herself as "funky and bam-bam’s auntie."

Extra special thank you to @sarrjamois for the best remote styling , @alastairmckimm for letting us wing it and my very very special Sister for making me feel beautiful. I love you lottt."
