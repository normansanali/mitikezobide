Itzan Escamilla (born October 31, 1998) is a Spanish Actor, Instagram Star, and Social Media Personality from Madrid. He first rose to fame after playing a starring role in a web series called Élite. It is a Spanish thriller teen drama web television program.

The first episode aired on the 5th of October 2018 on Netflix platform. Furthermore, the show was created by Carlos Montero and Darío Madrona. Itzan played one of the lead characters named Samuel García Domínguez in the series.

The hit show follows students and their friends who study at an elite secondary school. His character is one of the three transfer students. Samuel is also the love interest of Marina Nunier Osuna (performed by María Pedraza) and later Carla Rosón Caleruega developed a soft corner for him.

The role of Carla was acted by Ester Expósito. Some of the other main casts of Élite are Miguel Bernardeau as Guzmán Nunier Osuna, Claudia Salas as Rebeka “Rebe” de Bormujo Ávalos, Miguel Herrán as Christian Varela Expósito, etc.

Career, Various Shows & Net worth

In 2016, the actor entered the entertainment industry with a show called “Víctor Ros”. He performed as one of the recurring roles as Juan in seven episodes of the program. Moreover, he worked with actors like Carles Francino, Tomás del Estal, Paco Tous, etc.

Escamilla’s performance in this program led him to play a small part in the superhit TV soap opera called Seis Hermanas. He appeared in an episode called 1.281 as Gorrilla. The same year, the actor also starred in the “Centro Médico” show.

Later, Itzan Escamilla acted in an Adventure, Drama, History named El Final del Camino in 2017. His character “Simón Bolívar” appeared in an episode “Tiempo de ilustrados”.
