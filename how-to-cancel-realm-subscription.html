Does closing a realm delete it?

Closing the server just means nobody can connect to it while it is offline. All your data/saves etc. aren’t touched.

Can you pause a Realms subscription?

You can pause your Minecraft Realms subscription directly through the store in which you purchased it. For Minecraft Java edition, log into your Minecraft account and find your subscriptions listed there. They can all be paused by clicking on the Yes, Pause button next to your recurring payment.

What happens if you stop paying for a Minecraft realm?

If your subscription ends, you and your friends will no longer be able to play online together on Realms, and you will no longer have access to the packs or cloud storage.

How do I get a refund from Minecraft realms?

Refunds are available if you contact Minecraft Support within 15 days of purchase and if:
  • You made a duplicate purchase.
  • A purchase was made without your permission.
  • You’re unable to play your game after visiting our technical support resources.

    • How do I get a refund from Microsoft?

    To get a refund, you must visit the Microsoft account website with your web browser and navigate to Payment & Billing > Order History. Then, select the app or game and select “Request a refund.”

    How do I cancel a Microsoft subscription and get a refund?

    Go to the Services & subscriptions page for your account. Select Manage next to the subscription you want to cancel. In the Payment settings section, follow the steps to cancel your subscription. Next, choose between Don’t charge me on (next renewal date) and Cancel immediately and get a refund.

    How do I stop Microsoft from charging my card?

    Thanks for your feedback. If your card is attached to an active subscription that has auto renew on you would have to sign into account.live.com go to services and subscriptions and turn off the auto renew then go into payment options on the website and it would then allow you to remove your credit card.

    Can you refund a subscription?

    Can Apple refund a subscription?

    Problem with a subscription? You can request a refund. You can also cancel the subscription.

    How do I cancel an app subscription?

    Manage your subscriptions on Google Play
  • Open the Google Play app .
  • At the top right, tap the profile icon.
  • Tap Payments & subscriptions. Subscriptions.
  • Select the subscription you want to cancel.
  • Tap Cancel subscription.
  • Follow the instructions.
