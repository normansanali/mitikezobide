Boston Nealand Scott, a professional American Rocket League esports player for Dignitas, was born on April 27, 1995, and is 27 years old. He plays as a running back for the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League (NFL). In college, he played football for Louisiana Tech. In the sixth round of the 2018 NFL Draft, he was selected by the New Orleans Saints. He plays with players like Haason Reddick, and Zach Pascal amongst others.

The Philadelphia Eagles took Scott off the practice squad of the Saints on December 10, 2018. On August 31, 2019, Scott was released at the team’s final roster cuts, but the following day, he was re-signed to the practice squad.

On October 11, 2019, he received a promotion to the active roster. On October 27, 2019, the Eagles defeated the Bills 31-13, and Scott scored his first career touchdown on a 4-yard run.
