Happy National Nail Polish Day! Yes, that’s a real holiday — it was stared by Essie to celebrate all things nails, and we’re on board. Whether you enjoy a pampering appointment at the salon or a DIY paint job, there is no denying the power of a good mani/pedi. With the unofficial start of summer finally upon Us, it is time to swap out those rich burgundies and deep plums in favor of bright, playful colors that will remind you of a trip to the beach or relaxing day by the pool— even if you are stuck in the office.

While you can never go wrong with a poppy pink or vibrant orange, we’ve identified a few unexpected hues that are sure to be hot as the temperatures rise. From a pastel mellow yellow to a nautical-inspired navy, now is the time to take a fresh look at the coloring box and let your digits have some fun.
