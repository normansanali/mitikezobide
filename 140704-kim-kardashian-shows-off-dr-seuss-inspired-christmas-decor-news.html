Kim Kardashian Shows Off &quot;Dr. Seuss&quot; Inspired Christmas Decor

September 02, 2024
blog

Kim Kardashian and Kanye West had a lavish Christmas Eve party which was filled with a star-studded guest list that included John Legend, Dave Chapelle, Tyler, the Creator, Paris Hilton, and Chris Brown. Now Kim has taken to Instagram to show off some of the decor from the event and also give thanks to everyone who made it happen.

"Love seeing this decor. Very Dr. Seuss, Lorax, Grinch vibes. Creative Directed by Kanye. Thank you John Maguire at Trask House & @mindyweiss for always making Kanye’s vision come to life. The trees inside the house were done by @KathyTemin," Kim said in her post.

As you can see in the Instagram post above, the dining room hall where the party took place is completely dressed in white with fluffy accoutrements and some bright lighting. Just like Kim points out, the whole scene looks like something out of a Dr. Seuss book. 

According to People, the entire party cost about $1.3 million. The event itself was completely catered and even featured a performance from Legend who was there with his wife Chrissy Teigen.
