Kevin Sumlin Wiki Biography

Kevin Warren Sumlin (born August 3, 1964) is an American football coach and former player. He is currently the head football coach at Texas A&M University, a position he accepted in December 2011. Sumlin was the head football coach at the University of Houston from 2007 to 2011. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
