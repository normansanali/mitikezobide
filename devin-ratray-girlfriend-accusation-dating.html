Who Is Home Alone Star Devin Ratray? He Accused Of Physically Assaulting His Girlfriend

September 17, 2024

The Home alone star, Devin Ratray, was accused of physical assault by his girlfriend after they got into an argument.

According to TMZ reports, the alleged incident happened last week, 9 December and a woman filed for police report claiming that the actor, Devin Ratray punched her in the face, put his hand over her mouth, and tried to strangle her after a feud.

The TMZ was also informed by Law Enforcement sources that the couple went their separate ways for the night as things got calmed down after calling cops to a Hyatt hotel near downtown Oklahoma City. At last, cops left without anyone arrested, and things settled down between the couple, deciding on their split.

However, the actor’s rep denied any physical violence in a statement that also confirmed the couple’s breakup happened after the fight broke out.

Devin Ratray Dating History

The 44 years old Home Alone actor Devin Ratray is not married to date and has recently broken up with his girlfriend, whose identity is not revealed. 

Back in 2013, the actor had made a tweet about how he needed costume ideas for him and his girlfriend, but in 2015, the actor had made a tweet about his relationship status, saying, “My ex-girlfriend really didn’t know anything about movies. She became disillusioned when she realized.”

Who Is Devin Ratray?

Devin Ratray is a 44-year-old American actor who was born in 1977 and raised in New York, and started his career as a child actor. He had celebrity parents who were both actors, Peter Ratray and Ann Willis, and had connections in the industry which made him get some early roles. The actor graduated from New York’s famous ‘Florella H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts.’

The actor has a height of 6’1,” i.e., 185 cm.

Devin Ratray debuted at the age of nine as a child actor in the film Where Are the Children? and Home Alone in 1990 as the bully older brother of the main lead in the comedy Christmas movie. His other works are The Prince and Me, Dennis the Menace, Nebraska, Blue Ruin, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, etc.

Among his works, Home Alone remains the most famous and well-recognized one where he played the Buzz McCallister, the mean older brother to Kevin, played by Macaulay Culkin. The actor, Ratray, has revealed to People that the cast of Home Alone will host a reunion after the original Christmas flick aired 31 years ago. Last week he shared, “They have been planning a reunion online and I just recently, as in a couple of days ago, got on their messenger chat thread.”
