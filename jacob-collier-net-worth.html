Jacob Collier Biography

Jacob Collier is an English musician known for his work in The Boss Baby, The Best Man, and The Musical for A Christmas Carol. He is a multi-instrumentalist who has taken together more than 650,000 viewers for his acapella and instrumental arrangements on YouTube.
