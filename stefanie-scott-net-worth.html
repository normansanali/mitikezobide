Stefanie Scott is an American actress who started her career at age 12, both on television and film. Her first movie was Beethoven’s Big Break while her first notable role was in Flipped. At the same time, she was appearing as a guest star in a couple of television shows. She ultimately got her major break after landing a regular role on A.N.T. Farm as Lexi Reed. Simultaneously, she acted in the movies No Strings Attached, Frenemies, and starred in the horror feature Insidious: Chapter 3 as well as in Jem and the Holograms. Her latest stint on TV was in Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.
