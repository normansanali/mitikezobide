Is Estee Lauder cruelty free?

The Estée Lauder Companies were one of the first cosmetics companies to stop animal testing as a way of assessing cosmetic product safety more than 30 years ago. Estee Lauder claim to not test their products on animals, and to not hire third parties to do so either.

That being said, it is worth noting that Estee Lauder still sells its good in countries where animal testing on cosmetics is required by law. Peta therefore believes that by doing so, they are not a cruelty free brand. We think that everyone will have to make their own choice on this matter.
