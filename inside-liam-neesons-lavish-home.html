Inside Liam Neesons lavish home

September 18, 2024
blog

Liam Neeson has been one of Hollywood’s biggest stars for decades. Known for his appearances in Schindler’s List, The Grey, and Taken, Neeson has accrued plenty of accolades during his film career and also plenty of wealth to match.

As a star of this magnitude, it shouldn’t come as any surprise that Neeson owns a spectacular home. His residence in New York has it all, making it the perfect place for him and his family to relax and even stage a getaway without leaving the property.

If you’re after a look inside one of Hollywood’s most extravagant homes, here’s what we could find about Neeson’s impressive estate.

Liam Neeson’s New York estate

Liam Neeson’s Dutchess County, New York property has everything you’d expect for a star of his stature. The property spans 37 acres and takes full advantage of the natural landscape alongside the 200-year-old mansion.

The centerpiece of the estate, Neeson’s home is a colonial-style mansion of which the living space takes up over 6,000 feet. The house, which was reportedly built in 1810, has plenty of space, and each bedroom is equipped with an adjoining bathroom. It isn’t noted how many bedrooms are in the home, but from its original real estate listing, it seems that there are many along with plenty of living areas to relax and entertain.

Any movie star needs a place to watch a film in style, and Neeson’s home is of course equipped with a state-of-the-art home theater. Neeson can further relax by playing a game of golf without ever leaving the house thanks to the golf simulator inside. You can check out images of many of these features compliments of Realtor.com.

There is also what appears to be a full-size gym and massage room in the residence, so the actor can satisfy all of his fitness and recovery needs. You’ll also find an indoor pool with a stunning view of the property, all without leaving the comfort of the home.

As you head outside, you’ll find plenty more to experience. Of course, there’s an outdoor pool also for when Neeson prefers to do laps outside in the sun. There’s even a full-size tennis court to take advantage of alongside a modern patio area to sit and relax in the outdoor atmosphere. The property is also home to a lake just a short wander from the mansion and a large greenhouse where home-grown food is produced.

Further away from the main house is a pair of restored barns, with one of them home to the ultimate entertaining space. Inside, Neeson is said to have one of the most gnarly projector setups around, with a massive screen, ample speakers, and a top-of-the-line projector. Also inside is reportedly a game room and much more, making it anything but your typical barn ⏤ which makes sense, honestly, given that from his stellar career to his staggering net worth of $145 million, little about Neeson’s life can be classified as typical.
