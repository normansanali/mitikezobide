Cassadee Dunlap On Netflix’s Cheer Season 2

Cassadee Dunlap is one of the popular cheerleaders in her team because her appearance in TLC shows ‘Cheer Perfection’ and ‘Toddlers Tiaras’. She is one of the new faces that we’re going to see in season 2 of Netflix’s ‘Cheer’.

The Emmy-winning docuseries returns with the Navarro College cheerleaders. The first season of the show aired in January of 2020. Monica Aldama, Navarro’s coach, and the rest of her team became celebrities as a result of the show.

The show’s second season trailer also revealed that Jerry Harris, his arrest, and the aftermath of his arrest will be the focus of the 2022 season. The second season of ‘Cheer’ began filming in secret in January 2020, after the first season became an instant hit. The new season also features Navarro arch-rival Trinity Valley Community College’s coach and cheerleading squad.

The production was halted in March owing to the COVID-19 outbreak. However, filming resumed in September 2020. Along with the new castings, viewers will recognize Monica Aldama, Lexi Brumback, Gabi Butler, La’Darius Marshall, and Morgan Simianer from previous seasons. The show has nine episodes in its second season. This time is different. This time Navarro has to defend its title against its rival Trinity Valley Community College.

The events surrounding Jerry Harris are chronicled in the series from early 2020 to April 2021, with the charges against him being covered in episode five. The brothers who accused Jerry of sexual communication will also have their stories told on the show.

Other newbies besides Cassadee are Brooke Morosca, Gillian Rupert, Maddy Brum, and Payton Sykes.
