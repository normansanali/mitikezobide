What are Christmas Eve boxes?

Believed to have originated from the German tradition of opening presents on Christmas Eve, it's now become popular in the US and UK to get into the festive spirit early with a box of treats. Searches for Christmas Eve boxes increased by a huge 3000% last year, with families all over the country planning to take part and gift their own.

Often given in a personalised box or crate, you could make your own by upcycling something you already have, or alternatively you'll find lots of options from the likes of NotOnTheHighStreet and Etsy which can then be used year after year.
