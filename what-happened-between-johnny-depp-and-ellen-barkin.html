Barkin And Depp Got Close In 'Fear And Loathing In Las Vegas'

Ellen Barkin's time with Johnny Depp has been making waves lately, and the actress recently opened up about their former romance, even giving details about when things began between them.

"According to Barkin, she and Depp became friends in 1990 but ultimately, they got into a relationship, which was more of sexual nature than the romantic one in 1994. She reportedly said their “romance” lasted about 3-5 months and she “never heard from him again,” before also claiming that they “were romantically involved” when they worked together in the 1998 film ‘Fear & Loathing in Las Vegas.’ The film was shot on location in Las Vegas from August 3, 1997, and lasted 56 days," writes Meaww.

This is pretty typical in Hollywood, as many relationships start on the sets of movies. Even though Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas is known for being a chaotic flick, it is interesting to learn that this is when Depp and Barkin began their romantic relationship.

Another key part of her testimony that got people talking was the fact that she dished on Johnny Depp's jealousy.

Barkin said that Depp was "just a jealous man, controlling, where are you going, who are you going with. What did you do last night. I had a scratch on my back once that got him very, very angry because he insisted it came from having sex with a person who wasn't him.”
